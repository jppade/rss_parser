Description of the visualization of most frequent words

For the visualization, all words which appear with a frequency higher than the value entered as threshold are taken into consideration. Here, we take into account word appearence in all articles from a list of (around 39) RSS-feeds. The relations between the resulting words are visualized in a graph as follows: As soon as two words appear in the summary of an article, an edge (with weight one) is established between them. For every additional appearence in the summary of a different article, the weight is increased by one.


Word exclusion

For the count, we exclude a list of words such as "a", "an", "and", ...
